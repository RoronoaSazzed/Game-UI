how to play

1.

Guess the NOODLE using the four basic arithmetic operations applied to four randomly chosen numbers!

2.

You may use each of the four numbers originally selected once, and the result of each operation performed with them once.

3.

For example, if you multiply 5 by 2 to obtain 10, you may no longer use the 2 or 5, but may use the 10 in further calculations.

4.

It's not mandatory to use all the numbers. All numbers used must be integers.

Five new NOODLES will be available each day. You got this!
